## 🗝️ Foundational Principles

This repository explores several key axioms of mastery:

* **Competence vs. Confidence:** Competence is knowledge that can be taught from a book. Confidence is an internal belief forged only through stepping into difficult situations, owning mistakes, and proving to yourself that you can handle the friction.
* **The Samurai Way:** When a project goes sideways or a client is unhappy, do not deflect. Step directly into the blow, address the unspoken tension immediately, and take control of the resolution. 
* **The 15-Minute Rule:** Never struggle with a problem alone for more than 15 minutes. Ask for help. True professionals value finding the solution over protecting their ego.
* **Accountability as a Bond:** Take full ownership of your tasks, admit mistakes openly, and never throw your team under the bus. 
* **The Journey is the Reward:** You cannot work miserably for 40 years expecting a magical payoff at retirement. Fulfillment must be found in the day-to-day process of solving problems and helping people.
